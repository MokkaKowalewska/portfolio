For the past 10 years I've been booking ferry departures for cargo trucks. I may be a #CodeNewbie, but certainly not an employee newbie. In less that 5 years from an Assistant I became Senior Ferry Forwarding Agent and I've learnt a lot.

Long story short: I’m in my late 20s and I literally have no time to be a shitty developer, so I decided to be an awesome one. I am making it happen everyday, step by step. I know, I’m just in the beginning of my way and that this way actually never ends. And it’s even more exciting.
